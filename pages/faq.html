FAQ

Quick answers to common questions about how we work.

Do I keep the rights to my story?

Yes. We work under clear agreements where you retain ownership of your story. We license what we need to build and display it.

How long does a project take?

It depends on scope. A light edit and layout can be fast. A full comic build or deep ghostwriting takes longer. We give timelines after reviewing your project.

Do you accept every project?

No. We stay in a specific lane and only take on projects we believe we can elevate. If it’s not a fit, we’ll tell you honestly.

Can you help if my story isn’t finished?

Yes. We can come in at the idea, outline, or rough draft stage and help you build it into a finished story.

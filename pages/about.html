What Boardwalk Publishing Is

Boardwalk Publishing is a private, black-and-gold publishing house built for creators who want more than a generic upload button. It’s a place where comics, novels, and personal stories are treated like cinematic experiences.

The imprint was created to give authors and comic makers a space that feels intentional—soft cursive, falling feathers, gold dust, and quiet confidence. Every project is handled like a one-of-one piece.

From full comic production to ghostwriting and book display, Boardwalk Publishing is designed to be both a home for your own work and a platform for other voices you choose to bring in.

We work with:

  • Authors with finished or in-progress manuscripts
  • People who want their life turned into a comic or book
  • Creators who want a private, curated place to sell their work

Services include editing, formatting, comic scripting, visual planning, ghostwriting, and premium display slots on the site.

How Publishing Works

A clear, step‑by‑step path from idea to finished release.

Step 1 — Submit

You send us your story, outline, or concept through the Publish With Us page. We review it to see if it fits our lane and what level of build it needs.

Step 2 — Develop

We work with you on structure, pacing, and clarity. This is where we shape the story into something that can carry a full book or comic.

Step 3 — Design & Build

We handle editing, layout, typography, cover design, and comic production. This is where your story becomes a visual object, not just a document.

Step 4 — Release & Display

Once everything is approved, we release your work digitally and, if selected, display it in the Boardwalk catalog with a premium presentation.
